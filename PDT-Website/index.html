About Us.

We aim to create a tightly knit culture that revolves around having fun as well as upholding the core standards of Phi Delta Theta: scholarship, moral rectitude, and brotherhood, to foster the development of our members through active involvement in fraternal matters and accountability, & to recognize the uniqueness of our organization and its members.
